Why Georgia is the Best MBBS Abroad Option in 2025

June 8, 2025

Georgia has rapidly climbed the rankings among MBBS abroad destinations, and the reason is straightforward: it offers the broadest global degree recognition of any country at this price point.

The Recognition Advantage

A Georgian MBBS degree is valid for practice eligibility pathways in the EU, the United States (via ECFMG certification), the United Kingdom (via PLAB), and India (via FMGE/NEXT) — a combination unmatched by Russia, Central Asia, or most other popular destinations.

Safety and Quality of Life

Georgia consistently ranks among the world's safest countries in international indices, and Tbilisi offers a modern, walkable capital with a thriving international student community. The climate is notably milder than Russia or Kazakhstan, comparable to North India, easing the adjustment for new students.
