Our Story

From One Counsellor to India's Most Trusted Consultancy

MyAdmissionAdvisor was founded in 2004 with a simple mission: help every student who wants to become a doctor find a path, regardless of their NEET score or financial background. What started as a single counselling office has grown into a pan-India consultancy with a network spanning 400+ Indian colleges and 85+ universities across 8 countries.

We believe that a NEET score reflects a single exam on a single day — it should never be the sole determinant of whether a dedicated student gets to pursue medicine. Our counsellors evaluate each student's complete profile — academic history, financial situation, geographic preferences, and long-term career goals — to map out a realistic, achievable pathway.

What Sets Us Apart

Every university and college we recommend has been personally visited and vetted by our team. We don't just rely on brochures and marketing materials from institutions — we verify hostel quality, clinical training infrastructure, faculty credentials, and most importantly, actual FMGE pass rates for students returning from abroad universities.

Our Commitment to Transparency

The education consulting industry has, unfortunately, earned a reputation for hidden fees and undisclosed costs. We've built our reputation on the opposite approach: every fee, every cost, every requirement is disclosed upfront, in writing, before a family commits to anything. This transparency is why thousands of families trust us with one of the most important decisions of their child's life.
