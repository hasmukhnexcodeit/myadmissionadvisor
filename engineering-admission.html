Frequently Asked Questions

What engineering branches have the best job prospects? +
Computer Science Engineering consistently leads in placement rates and starting salaries, followed by Information Technology and Electronics & Communication, driven by India's growing tech industry.
Can I get engineering admission with a low JEE score? +
Yes. Management quota admissions are available in 300+ private engineering colleges across India for students who don't qualify for their preferred branch or college through JEE Main alone.
What is the fee range for engineering management quota seats? +
Fees typically range from ₹1.5 lakhs to ₹6 lakhs per year depending on the branch and college reputation, with Computer Science generally commanding the highest fees.
